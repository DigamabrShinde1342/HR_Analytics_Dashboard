# HR Analytics Dashboard — Power BI Project

## 📌 Project Overview

The **HR Analytics Dashboard** is an executive-level workforce intelligence solution developed in **Power BI** to analyze employee attrition, workforce demographics, job satisfaction, and organizational risk factors.

This project transforms raw HR data into actionable business insights that help leadership teams improve retention strategies, workforce planning, and employee engagement.

The dashboard provides a complete analytical view of:

* Employee attrition trends
* Department-wise workforce behavior
* Job satisfaction analysis
* Demographic segmentation
* Workforce risk identification
* Strategic HR decision-making

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

Organizations often struggle with:

* High employee attrition
* Rising recruitment and training costs
* Lack of centralized HR reporting
* Limited visibility into workforce risks
* Reactive rather than proactive HR decisions

This project addresses these challenges by creating an interactive HR analytics dashboard that enables data-driven workforce management.

# 📈 Key Visualizations

## 1. Employee Distribution by Age & Gender

* Workforce segmentation across different age groups
* Male vs Female employee comparison
* Identification of high-density workforce segments

### Insight:

Employees aged **25–34** represent the largest workforce segment and show the highest attrition exposure.

---

## 2. Attrition by Age

* Tracks attrition patterns across age ranges
* Identifies vulnerable employee age groups

### Insight:

Young and mid-career employees are more likely to leave the organization.

---

## 3. Department-wise Attrition

* Attrition comparison across departments:

  * R&D
  * Sales
  * HR

### Insight:

The **R&D department** recorded the highest attrition volume, potentially impacting innovation continuity.

---

## 4. Attrition Rate by Job Role

Highlights high-risk job roles.

### Major Findings:

| Job Role              | Attrition Rate |
| --------------------- | -------------- |
| Sales Representative  | 39.76%         |
| Laboratory Technician | 23.94%         |
| Human Resources       | 23.08%         |

### Insight:

Customer-facing and operational roles experience significantly higher attrition.

---

## 5. Job Satisfaction Analysis

Analyzes employee satisfaction scores across job roles.

### Insight:

Lower satisfaction ratings strongly correlate with employee exits.

---

## 6. Attrition by Education Field

Identifies educational backgrounds with high attrition levels.

### Insight:

Employees from **Life Sciences** and **Medical** backgrounds show higher attrition numbers.

---

# 🔍 Key Business Insights

* The **25–34 age group** shows the highest workforce mobility risk.
* **Sales Representatives** have the highest attrition rate.
* Frequent business travel contributes to burnout and employee turnover.
* Low job satisfaction is directly associated with increased attrition.
* Workforce attrition is concentrated in operational and customer-facing roles.

# 💡 Strategic Recommendations

## Retention Strategy

* Launch targeted retention programs for high-risk roles.

## Workforce Planning
